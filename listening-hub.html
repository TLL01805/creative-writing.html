Beginner

Topic: Ordering Food at a Cafe

Listening Script: Read aloud or have a partner read this conversation to you:

"Hello! Welcome to Sunshine Cafe. What can I get for you today?"
"Hi! I would like a hot cup of coffee and a chocolate muffin, please."
"Sure thing! Will that be all for you?"
"Yes, thank you. How much is it?"
"That will be five dollars, please."

Quick Question: What pastry did the customer order?

Answer: A chocolate muffin! 🧁
Intermediate

Topic: Changing Weather Patterns

Listening Script: Listen to the news report script below:

"Good morning listeners. Meteorologists are reporting an unexpected shift in local weather systems this week. Unseasonably high temperatures are causing regional reservoirs to drop faster than projected. Citizens are urged to conserve water resources until heavy precipitation arrives next Tuesday."

Vocabulary Check: What does the word "precipitation" mean in this report?

Meaning: Rain or snow falling to the ground! 🌧️
Advanced

Topic: The Economics of Renewable Energy

Listening Script: Analyze the academic audio transcript extract:

"The transitional paradigm toward sustainable infrastructure is fundamentally tethered to market viability. While initial capital expenditure for clean energy matrices remains substantially prohibitive for developing economies, long-term operational yields demonstrate clear systemic superiority over traditional fossil fuels."

Vocabulary Check: What does the word "prohibitive" imply here?

Meaning: The cost is too high, making it difficult or impossible to buy! 💰
